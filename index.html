The best of

ATARI XL/XE
demoscene music
vol. 1

All tracks on this album were recorded directly through an analog signal path from a real 8bit Atari computer with two original POKEY chips, and then analog-mastered at EPROM SOUNDS STUDIO. We do believe this effort was worth it, as it allowed us to preserve the soul of this wonderful music.

Click on the track list to play a snippet of the mastered track.
